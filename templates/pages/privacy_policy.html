Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how RegMarket ("we", "us", "the Service") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information in connection with our store management system.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly and information collected automatically when you use the Service.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: name, email address, password, profile photo, and other profile fields.
  • Store data: products, inventory, sales records, purchases, invoices, and other data you or your users upload to manage the store.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Log data (e.g., IP address, access timestamps, browser and device information).
  • Usage data required to operate and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service;
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts;
  • Process and store store-management data required for the Service to function;
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to security incidents or abuse;
  • Improve and develop new features.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

RegMarket does not sell or trade personal information. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or payment services as part of the current Service. We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal and account data as long as necessary to provide the Service and for a reasonable period afterward for operational, legal, or security purposes. In general:

  • Active account data is retained while the account exists.
  • After account deletion, we will remove personal data from active systems and retain backups and limited records for up to 12 months to address operational needs and comply with legal obligations, unless a different retention period is required by law.

5. Account Deletion and Data Access

Account owners may request deletion of their account and personal data by contacting us at the address below. Upon request we will delete account data from active systems and remove personal identifiers in accordance with this policy, subject to any legal or operational retention obligations. Users may also request access to their personal data and a copy of their account data.

6. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Marketing

RegMarket does not send marketing or promotional emails to users. If this changes in the future, users will be given the opportunity to opt-in or opt-out as applicable.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will post the updated policy and update the effective date. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.

10. Contact
